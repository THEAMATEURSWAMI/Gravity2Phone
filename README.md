# Antigravity Mobile Bridge

Building a voice-first, push-notification integrated bridge to turn your mobile device into a remote command center for your high-performance development environment.

## The Vision
Stop viewing your phone as a small computer for typing, and start viewing it as a **Command Pager**. 
- **Voice-first**: No tiny keyboards. Speak your intent.
- **Proactive Notifications (The "Ding")**: Your dev environment calls *you* when it's done or needs help.
- **Secure by Default**: Powered by Tailscale, keeping your "Brain" invisible to the public internet.
